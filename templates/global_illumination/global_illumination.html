Welcome to my website! This site serves as a platform for submitting assignments for my Global Illuminations course, which focuses on ray tracing. In this course, we work through multiple checkpoints to gradually build up our skills and replicate complex images using ray tracing techniques. Each submission corresponds to one of these checkpoints, where I present the work completed and explain the process I followed to achieve the final result. Ray tracing is a rendering technique that simulates the way light interacts with objects in a virtual scene, producing highly realistic images. Throughout the course, I will be learning various aspects of ray tracing, from understanding light behavior to optimizing performance for faster rendering. The assignments on this website will showcase my progress as I work toward mastering this powerful tool.
